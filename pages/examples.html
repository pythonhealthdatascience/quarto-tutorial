Examples

Research projects

Projects that document and share a single, well-defined piece of research, usually with its own paper, report, or case study, plus code and/or interactive outputs.

Computational Reproducibility Assessments: Summary
University of Exeter
Summary of eight computational reproducibility assessments from STARS Work Package 1, presented as a detailed digital appendix to the paper with full methods, results, tables, and figures.
Exeter Oncology Model: Renal Cell Carcinoma edition (EOM-RCC)
University of Exeter
Documentation for EOM-RCC,a platform cost-effectiveness model encompassing each decision node in the disease area for advanced renal cell carcinoma.
nordatlantisk-ft
Website for an open voting-records dataset, with a data overview, detailed codebook of all variables, and information about the repository.
Simulating Attention and Performance tasks with an LLM
R project recording various simulation experiments.
NAture-positive SCenarios for ENvironmental Transitions: Peru
Project website with maps, photo galleries, interactive figures, updates.
Towards an open-source model for data and metadata standards
Online report with background/methods/results and links to extra pages providing more details.
research-compendium-website
Template site with pages about, data, codes and manuscript.
PLB DSS Analysis
Example using the template site, reporting on potate late blight forecasting model.
Is Latitude Associated with Chronotype?
Masters Thesis as quarto website.

Homepage for research programme or team

Overarching homepages that present an entire project, programme, lab, or community, bringing together people, research, resources, news, and key links in one place.

STARS
University of Exeter
Summary site for the STARS research project, with dedicated pages describing each component of the project and separate pages for every associated publication, including links to code and embedded papers.
HSMA
University of Exeter
Programme homepage describing the HSMA training scheme, with resources, guidance, news, and pages on each and every HSMA student project.
Affective Communication & Computing Lab
Lab website with blog posts containing latest news, and with pages listing the team, research themes, publications, resources and opportunities.
Computational Cognition Lab
Research group site listing publications, people, opportunities. Also has latest news and links to their learning resources, including open textbooks and teaching materials built with tools like Bookdown and Quarto.
Digital Humanities Bern
Centre homepage with news and event listings, and individual pages on their research areas and services.
Hertie Coding Club
Club site introducing the group's mission, events, and workshops, with interactive resources, coding materials, and a polished, animated homepage experience.
Masiello Research Group
Group homepage with detailed researcher profiles, plus research area overviews, publications, software links, and news.
NHS-R Community
Community site sharing reports, blog posts, events, resources, and information about fellows and contributors, supporting users of R across the NHS.
Deep Energy and Climate Policy Lab
Group homagepage with blog posts, project summaries, publications, team, events and more.
Atlas of Living Australia Labs
Features analysis articles and data visualisations.
Real World Data Science
Project from the Royal Statistical Society - site contains mostly blog posts.
epiforecasts
Research group at the Centre for Mathematical Modelling of Infectious Diseases of the Longon School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine.
seedcase
Project website with roadmap, info on team, and links out to other sites focused on community and design.
openscapes
Clearly designed project website with great graphics and design.

Conferences and events

Event and seminar websites that share programmes, schedules, speakers, and materials for conferences, workshops, and talk series, often with links to slides, recordings, and related resources.

Package documentation

Use Quarto (with tools like quartodoc) to build clean, navigable documentation sites for your Python or R packages, including API reference, usage guides, and examples.

lintquarto
University of Exeter
Python package which runs linters and type checkers on code embedded in Quarto .qmd files.
vidigi
University of Exeter
Python package for creating animated visualisations of queues and pathways from simulation or real-world data.
skimpy
Python package providing summary statistics from dataframes.
